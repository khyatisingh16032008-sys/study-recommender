How It Works

The AI concepts powering every recommendation.

01

Intelligent Agent

The system models a classic AI agent with four roles: Perceive (reads your preferences), Brain (applies search + ranking), Action (recommends materials), and Learn (updates from feedback).

02

Search Strategy

Constraint-based filtering — like a SQL WHERE clause — matches your exact subject, difficulty, and type preferences against the materials database.

WHERE subject=? AND difficulty=? AND type=?
03

Heuristic Ranking

After filtering, materials are ranked by a weighted formula that favours challenging content and active learning types.

score = adaptive × d_weight × t_weight
Easy→1.0 · Medium→1.1 · Hard→1.2
Video→1.0 · Notes→1.05 · Practice→1.1
04

Adaptive Learning

Every rating you give feeds back into the score. More positive ratings push a material up; low ratings push it down — mimicking reinforcement learning.

adaptive = (base + Σ ratings) / (1 + N)

30 curated study materials across 4 subjects, 3 difficulty levels, and 3 types — all starting with a base score of 3.5 that evolves with your feedback.
